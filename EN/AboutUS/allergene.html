Allergens

Our cupcakes may contain the following allergens:

A - Cereals containing gluten

(such as wheat, rye, barley, oats, spelt, kamut, or their hybrid strains) and products made from them.

C - Eggs and products thereof

E - Peanuts and products thereof

F - Soybeans and products thereof

M - Milk and products thereof (including lactose)

H - Tree nuts

(Nuts such as almonds, hazelnuts, walnuts, cashews, pecans, Brazil nuts, pistachios, macadamias, and Queensland nuts) and products made from them.

We take great care to process allergenic ingredients separately. However, cross-contamination in our kitchen cannot be completely excluded, so traces of these allergens may be present.

Additional Labels

Gluten-Free - The product contains no gluten (< 20 mg/kg) and is suitable for people with gluten intolerance or celiac disease.

Lactose-Free - The product contains no digestible lactose (< 0.1 g/100 g or ml) and is therefore suitable for lactose-intolerant individuals.

Vegan - The product contains no animal ingredients or substances derived from animals (e.g., milk, eggs, honey, or gelatin).

Notice: Despite the greatest care in preparation, unintentional traces of allergens cannot be fully excluded. If you have allergies or intolerances, please contact our staff.
